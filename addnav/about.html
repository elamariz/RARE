About Company

Introducing ACE Tech, the innovative powerhouse driving the evolution of dormitory searches through ADDNAV.com. Our mission is clear: to streamline the process, alleviate financial burdens, and elevate the student living experience to new heights.

At ACE Tech, we're not just about helping students find their perfect dormitory; we're also committed to empowering dormitory owners. By maximizing their exposure and optimizing investments, we ensure mutual success and sustainability.
