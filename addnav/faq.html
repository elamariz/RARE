FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about ADDNAV. We're gonna help you find the answer!

What should I do if I encounter any issues with a dormitory listed on ADDNAV.com?

If you encounter any issues with a dormitory listed on ADDNAV.com, please contact us immediately through our website or email, and we will assist you in resolving the matter promptly.

Are there any fees for using ADDNAV.com?

No, using ADDNAV.com is completely free for students. However, dormitory owners may be subject to listing fees.

Can I contact dormitory owners through ADDNAV.com?

Yes, you can contact dormitory owners directly through the contact information provided on each listing.

How often is the information on ADDNAV.com updated?

We strive to keep the information on ADDNAV.com as up-to-date as possible. Our team regularly reviews and updates listings to ensure accuracy.

Can I leave reviews for dormitories on ADDNAV.com?

Yes, you can leave reviews for dormitories you have stayed in to help other students make informed decisions.
